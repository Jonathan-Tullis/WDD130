Explore Our Rivers

At Dry Oar, we take you down some of the most breathtaking and exhilarating rivers in the region. Whether you’re looking for peaceful scenic floats or heart-pounding rapids, we’ve got the river adventure for you.

Snake River

The Snake River winds through majestic canyons and beautiful wilderness. It's ideal for families and first-time rafters with stretches of calm waters and occasional class II rapids.

Colorado River

Experience the power of the Colorado River as it cuts through the Grand Canyon. Perfect for thrill-seekers, these class IV and V rapids are a bucket list adventure.

Salmon River

Known as the "River of No Return," the Salmon offers both adventure and tranquility. Enjoy multiday trips with riverside camping and starlit skies.
